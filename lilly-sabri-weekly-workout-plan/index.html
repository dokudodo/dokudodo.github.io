What Is Lilly Sabri's Weekly Workout Plan?

Lilly Sabri's weekly workout plan is a comprehensive fitness program that consists of five workouts per week. Each workout is designed to target different muscle groups and improve overall fitness. The workouts are designed to be challenging, but also accessible to people of all fitness levels. The program includes a mix of strength training and cardio, as well as some flexibility and mobility work.

How to Get Started with Lilly Sabri's Weekly Workout Plan?

If you want to get started with Lilly Sabri's weekly workout plan, the first step is to sign up for the program on her website. Once you've signed up, you'll receive access to the program's workout videos and other resources. Before you start the program, it's important to assess your current fitness level and make sure that you're healthy enough to engage in regular exercise.

Once you've assessed your fitness level, you can start following the program's workout schedule. The program consists of five workouts per week, with two rest days. Each workout is designed to be 45-60 minutes long, so you should be able to fit them into your schedule without too much difficulty.

Step-by-Step Guide to Lilly Sabri's Weekly Workout Plan

Here's a step-by-step guide to following Lilly Sabri's weekly workout plan:

Step 1: Warm Up

Before each workout, it's important to warm up properly. Lilly Sabri recommends doing some light cardio, such as jumping jacks or jogging in place, followed by some dynamic stretching exercises to prepare your muscles for the workout.

Step 2: Strength Training

The first part of each workout is dedicated to strength training. Lilly Sabri's workouts typically include a mix of bodyweight exercises, such as squats and lunges, as well as some weighted exercises, such as dumbbell curls and shoulder presses. The exercises are designed to target different muscle groups and help you build strength and endurance.

Step 3: Cardio

After the strength training portion of the workout, it's time for some cardio. Lilly Sabri's workouts typically include some high-intensity interval training (HIIT) exercises, such as burpees and mountain climbers, as well as some low-impact cardio, such as jumping jacks or step-ups. The cardio portion of the workout is designed to improve your cardiovascular fitness and burn calories.

Step 4: Flexibility and Mobility

Finally, each workout ends with some flexibility and mobility work. This is designed to help you improve your range of motion and reduce your risk of injury. Lilly Sabri's workouts typically include some yoga-inspired stretches and other mobility exercises.

Tips for Following Lilly Sabri's Weekly Workout Plan

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of Lilly Sabri's weekly workout plan:

  • Make sure to warm up properly before each workout.
  • Start with light weights and gradually increase the intensity as you get stronger.
  • Listen to your body and take rest days if you need them.
  • Stay hydrated and fuel your body with healthy, nutritious foods.
  • Don't be afraid to modify the exercises if you need to.
