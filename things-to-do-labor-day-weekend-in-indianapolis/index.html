Labor Day weekend is just around the corner, and it's time to start planning your activities. Indianapolis has plenty to offer during this holiday weekend, so we've put together a list of things to do.

Table of Contents

  • Outdoor Activities
  • Food and Drinks
  • Art and Culture
  • Family-Friendly Activities

Outdoor Activities

Indianapolis has plenty of outdoor activities to keep you busy during Labor Day weekend. Here are a few ideas:

Paddle the Canal

The Canal Walk is a beautiful place to take a stroll, but why not take it a step further and rent a paddleboat or kayak? You can enjoy the water and the sights at the same time.

Visit the White River State Park

The White River State Park is a great place to spend the day with family and friends. You can take a walk, go on a bike ride, have a picnic, or visit some of the attractions located in the park.

Food and Drinks

Indianapolis is known for its food scene, and Labor Day weekend is the perfect time to indulge. Here are some places to check out:

Indy Burger Week

If you're a burger lover, you won't want to miss Indy Burger Week. From August 29th to September 4th, you can try special burgers at over 40 restaurants around the city.

Brewery Tours

Indianapolis has a great craft beer scene, and Labor Day weekend is the perfect time to go on a brewery tour. Some of the top breweries in the city include Sun King Brewery, Indiana City Brewing, and Flat12 Bierwerks.

Art and Culture

If you're looking for something a little more cultural, Indianapolis has plenty of options. Here are a few ideas:

Indianapolis Museum of Art

The Indianapolis Museum of Art is a great place to spend the day. You can explore the galleries, see the outdoor art installations, or take a walk in the gardens.

Art in the Park

On September 2nd, you can visit Garfield Park for Art in the Park. This event features over 50 local artists, live music, and food trucks.

Family-Friendly Activities

If you're looking for activities to do with your family, Indianapolis has plenty of options. Here are a few ideas:

Indiana State Fair

The Indiana State Fair runs from August 5th to September 4th, so Labor Day weekend is your last chance to check it out. There are rides, games, and plenty of food to enjoy.

Indianapolis Children's Museum

The Indianapolis Children's Museum is a great place to take your kids. They can explore the exhibits, play in the outdoor space, and even climb a giant dinosaur.

These are just a few of the things to do in Indianapolis during Labor Day weekend. Whatever you choose to do, make sure to enjoy the holiday and spend time with family and friends.
