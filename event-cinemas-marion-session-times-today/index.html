Description

Event Cinemas Marion is a popular cinema located in the Marion Shopping Centre in Adelaide, South Australia. The cinema boasts 18 screens, including four Vmax screens and two Gold Class screens. The cinema offers a range of movies, from blockbuster hits to independent films.

How to

To find out the session times for Event Cinemas Marion today, simply head to the cinema's website. On the homepage, you'll see a "Movies & Times" tab. Click on this tab and select "Marion" from the drop-down menu. This will take you to a page with all the session times for the cinema today.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to finding the session times for Event Cinemas Marion today:

  1. Go to the Event Cinemas website
  2. Click on the "Movies & Times" tab
  3. Select "Marion" from the drop-down menu
  4. Choose the movie you want to see
  5. Select the session time that suits you

Tips

  • Bring your own snacks and drinks to save money
