What is After Hours Song About?

"After Hours" is a song by Canadian singer The Weeknd. The song is from his fourth studio album of the same name. The song was released on March 20, 2020, as the lead single from the album. The song is about The Weeknd's experiences of being in a toxic relationship. The lyrics of the song imply that the relationship was not healthy and had a negative impact on his life.

The song is an emotional rollercoaster that explores the feelings of love, hurt, and betrayal. The Weeknd's vocals are raw and emotional, perfectly capturing the pain and confusion that comes with a toxic relationship. The song has a haunting melody that will stay with you long after you listen to it.

The Inspiration Behind the Song

The Weeknd has been known for his introspective and personal lyrics. In an interview with Rolling Stone, he revealed that the song is based on his real-life experiences. He said, "It was about a relationship that I was in, it was toxic. And I was just trying to escape it. And I think the After Hours represents the journey that I had to go through to find the happiness that I have now."

The Weeknd has been open about his struggles with addiction and has spoken about how his past relationships have contributed to it. He has said that the song is a reflection of his journey towards sobriety and finding true happiness.

The Lyrics of After Hours Song

The lyrics of "After Hours" are powerful and emotional. The song starts with The Weeknd singing, "Thought I almost died in my dream again / Fightin' for my life, I couldn't breathe again." The lyrics suggest that he was struggling to break free from a toxic relationship.

The chorus of the song is equally powerful, with The Weeknd singing, "I'm blinded by the lights / No, I can't sleep until I feel your touch." The lyrics suggest that he is still in love with the person he was in the toxic relationship with, despite the negative impact it had on his life.

The Music Video

The music video for "After Hours" is a visual masterpiece. The video is shot in a first-person perspective and follows The Weeknd as he navigates through a city at night. The video is filled with symbolism and hidden messages, and it has become a fan favorite.

The video has received critical acclaim, with many praising its cinematic quality. The video perfectly captures the mood and tone of the song, and it is a must-watch for any fan of The Weeknd.

Final Thoughts

"After Hours" is a song that has captured the hearts of millions of fans around the world. The song is a testament to The Weeknd's talent as a songwriter and performer. The song is emotional, raw, and honest, and it is a must-listen for anyone who has ever been in a toxic relationship.
