1. Check Your Mailbox

Every day, you'll receive mail in your mailbox. Make sure to check it for important items like gifts from your friends, letters from your villagers, and Nook Miles rewards.


2. Check the Nook Stop Terminal

The Nook Stop Terminal is located in Resident Services. Check it every day for Nook Miles rewards, special promotions, and to check your bank account balance.


3. Check Turnip Prices

If you bought turnips, make sure to check the turnip prices every day. You may be able to sell them for a profit!


4. Check the Daily Specials at Nook’s Cranny

Nook’s Cranny has daily specials that change every day. Make sure to check them out to see what deals you can get!


5. Check Your Island for Fossils and Dig Them Up

Fossils are buried all over your island. Make sure to check for them every day and donate them to the museum or sell them for bells.


6. Check for DIY Recipes on the Beach

DIY recipes wash up on the beach every day. Make sure to check for them so you can add new items to your collection.


7. Check for Items in the Trees

Items like furniture and bells can sometimes be found in the trees on your island. Shake them every day to see what falls out!


8. Check for Villagers’ Birthdays

Make sure to check the bulletin board in Resident Services for villagers’ birthdays. You don't want to miss out on the celebration!


9. Check for Visitors

Visitors like Gulliver or K.K. Slider may visit your island. Make sure to check for them and see what they have to offer!


10. Fishing

Fishing is a great way to catch fish and make bells. Make sure to fish every day to add to your collection.


11. Catch Bugs

Catching bugs is another way to add to your collection and make bells. Make sure to catch as many as you can every day.


12. Water Your Flowers

Watering your flowers helps them grow and bloom. Make sure to water them every day to keep them healthy.


13. Sell Your Unwanted Items

If you have items you don't need, sell them at Nook’s Cranny or to other players. This is a great way to make bells and free up space in your inventory.


14. Donate to the Museum

If you catch fish or bugs, make sure to donate them to the museum. The museum is a great place to learn about the creatures on your island.


15. Talk to Your Villagers

Make sure to talk to your villagers every day to see if they have any tasks for you or to just chat. Building relationships with your villagers is an important part of the game.


16. Send Letters or Gifts to Your Friends

If you have friends who play Animal Crossing, send them letters or gifts to show them you're thinking of them.


17. Check on Your Turnips

If you bought turnips, make sure to check on them every day to see if you can sell them for a profit.


18. Create Custom Designs

Use the Custom Design app on your NookPhone to create custom designs for clothing or to decorate your island.


19. Redecorate Your Home or Island

If you're feeling bored with your home or island, redecorate! Move furniture around, add new items, and make your island your own.


20. Participate in Events

Animal Crossing has many events throughout the year. Make sure to participate to get special items and to have fun.


21. Visit Other Islands

If you have friends who play Animal Crossing, visit their islands to see what they've been up to and to get inspiration for your own island.


22. Complete Daily Nook Miles Tasks

Every day, there are new Nook Miles tasks to complete. Make sure to complete them to earn Nook Miles and to level up.


23. Use Your NookPhone Apps

Your NookPhone has many apps that can help you in the game. Make sure to use them every day to make the most of your time on your island.


24. Play with Friends

Animal Crossing is a social game. Play with your friends to share ideas, trade items, and have fun.


25. Relax and Enjoy Your Island

At the end of the day, Animal Crossing is a game meant to be enjoyed. Don't forget to take a moment to relax and enjoy your island.


Conclusion

With so many things to do every day in Animal Crossing New Horizons, you'll never run out of things to do. Whether you're completing tasks, catching fish, or just relaxing on your island, there's always something to keep you busy. Enjoy your time in the game and make the most of it!
