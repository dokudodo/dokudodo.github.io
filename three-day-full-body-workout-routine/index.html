How to Start a Three Day Full Body Workout Routine

Before starting any workout routine, it is important to consult with a doctor or a fitness professional to make sure that it is safe for you to do so. Once you have the green light, you can start planning your three day full body workout routine.

The first step is to set a goal for yourself. This can be anything from losing weight to building muscle or simply improving overall fitness. Once you have a goal, you can start planning your workout routine.

Next, you should decide on the exercises that you want to include in your routine. A full body workout routine should include exercises that target all major muscle groups, such as your chest, back, legs, and arms.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Three Day Full Body Workout Routine

Here is a sample three day full body workout routine:

Day 1: Upper Body

  • Bench press
  • Standing military press
  • Pull-ups
  • Chin-ups
  • Bicep curls
  • Tricep extensions
  • Dumbbell rows
  • Dumbbell flyes

Day 2: Lower Body

  • Squats
  • Deadlifts
  • Lunges
  • Calf raises
  • Leg press
  • Hamstring curls

Day 3: Full Body

  • Burpees
  • Mountain climbers
  • Kettlebell swings
  • Plank
  • Side plank
  • Leg raises
  • Crunches

It’s important to remember that this is just a sample routine. You can change up the exercises and the order in which you do them to fit your needs and preferences.

Tips for a Successful Three Day Full Body Workout Routine

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your three day full body workout routine:

  • Warm up before each workout to prevent injury.
  • Start with light weights and gradually increase the weight as you get stronger.
  • Allow for rest days in between workouts to give your muscles time to recover.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein to support muscle growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.

The Three Day Full Body Workout Routine: A Solution for Busy Schedules

The three day full body workout routine is an excellent choice for people with busy schedules. With just three workouts per week, you can get a full body workout without spending hours at the gym.

Additionally, this routine is easy to customize to fit your schedule. You can choose to do your workouts in the morning, afternoon, or evening, depending on what works best for you.

FAQs About the Three Day Full Body Workout Routine

Q: Is the three day full body workout routine suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, this routine is perfect for beginners who are looking to get into shape.

Q: Can I do cardio on the same day as my three day full body workout?

A: Yes, you can do cardio on the same day as your workout, but it’s important to make sure that you don’t overdo it. Start with a light cardio session and gradually increase the intensity as you get stronger.

Q: How long should my three day full body workout be?

A: Your workout should last between 45 minutes to an hour, depending on your fitness level and the intensity of your workout.

Pros and Cons of the Three Day Full Body Workout Routine

Pros:

  • Great for beginners
  • Easy to customize
  • Perfect for busy schedules
  • Targets all major muscle groups

Cons:

  • May not be ideal for advanced weightlifters
  • May not allow for as much specialization in certain muscle groups

The three day full body workout routine is an excellent choice for anyone looking to get into shape, lose weight, and improve overall fitness. It’s easy to customize, perfect for busy schedules, and targets all major muscle groups. With dedication and consistency, you can achieve your fitness goals with this routine.
