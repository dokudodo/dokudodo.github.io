Ways to Hang Posters - Tips, Step by Step Guide, Pros and Cons

If you are looking for ways to decorate your room or office, hanging posters is a great idea. Posters can add color and personality to any space. However, hanging posters can be tricky if you don't know how to do it properly. In this article, we will discuss different ways to hang posters and the pros and cons of each method.

Table of Contents

  1. Method 1: Using Tape
  2. Method 2: Using Poster Putty
  3. Method 3: Using Poster Hangers
  4. Method 4: Using Frames

Method 1: Using Tape

Using tape is the most common and easiest way to hang posters. All you need is some double-sided tape or masking tape. Here are the steps:

  1. Clean the surface where you want to hang the poster.
  2. Cut a small piece of tape and place it on the back of the poster.
  3. Stick the poster to the wall and press firmly.
  4. Repeat the process for all corners and edges of the poster.

Make sure to use a tape that won't damage the wall or the poster. Also, avoid using too much tape as it can leave residue on the wall or tear the poster when you remove it.

Method 2: Using Poster Putty

Poster putty is a reusable and non-damaging adhesive that is perfect for hanging posters. Here's how to use it:

  1. Clean the surface where you want to hang the poster.
  2. Take a small piece of putty and roll it into a ball.
  3. Stick the putty to the back of the poster at each corner.
  4. Press the poster firmly onto the wall.

Poster putty is easy to remove and won't leave any residue on the wall or the poster. It's also reusable, so you can use it again and again.

Method 3: Using Poster Hangers

Poster hangers are plastic strips that attach to the top and bottom edges of the poster. They come with adhesive backings that stick to the wall. Here's how to use them:

  1. Clean the surface where you want to hang the poster.
  2. Attach the poster hangers to the top and bottom edges of the poster.
  3. Peel off the adhesive backing and stick the hangers to the wall.

Poster hangers are great for larger posters as they distribute the weight evenly and prevent the poster from curling. However, they can be more expensive than other methods.

Method 4: Using Frames

Using frames is a more permanent and professional way to hang posters. Here's how to do it:

  1. Purchase a frame that fits your poster.
  2. Remove the back of the frame and insert the poster.
  3. Replace the back of the frame and hang it on the wall using a nail or hook.

Frames protect the poster from damage and give it a polished look. However, they can be expensive and may not be suitable for all posters.

Pros and Cons of Different Methods

Each method of hanging posters has its pros and cons. Here's a summary:

  • Using tape: easy and inexpensive, but can damage the wall or the poster.
  • Using poster putty: reusable and non-damaging, but may not hold heavier posters.
  • Using poster hangers: distribute weight evenly, but can be more expensive.
  • Using frames: protect the poster and give it a professional look, but can be expensive and may not be suitable for all posters.

FAQs About Hanging Posters

1. Can I use regular tape to hang posters?

Yes, you can use double-sided tape or masking tape to hang posters. However, make sure to use a tape that won't damage the wall or the poster.

2. How do I remove tape residue from the wall?

You can remove tape residue from the wall using a mixture of warm water and dish soap. Apply the mixture to the residue and let it sit for a few minutes. Then, scrub the residue with a sponge or cloth until it comes off.

3. Can I reuse poster putty?

Yes, poster putty is reusable. You can simply remove it from the poster and roll it into a ball again.

4. Can I hang posters without damaging the wall?

Yes, you can hang posters without damaging the wall by using poster putty or poster hangers.

5. Can I hang posters on a textured wall?

Yes, you can hang posters on a textured wall using any of the methods mentioned in this article. However, make sure to press the poster firmly onto the wall to ensure it sticks properly.

Now that you know different ways to hang posters, you can decorate your space with ease. Whether you choose to use tape, putty, hangers, or frames, make sure to choose the method that works best for your poster and your space. Happy decorating!
