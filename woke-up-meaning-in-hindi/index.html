Definition of Woke Up

Woke up is a phrasal verb that means to awaken from sleep or unconsciousness. It can also refer to becoming aware of something or having a sudden realization or understanding of a situation. The term "woke" is a past tense and past participle form of the verb "wake," which means to emerge from a state of sleep or unconsciousness.

The Meaning of Woke Up in Hindi

In Hindi, the term "woke up" can be translated to "जाग उठा" (jag utha). This phrase is commonly used in Hindi to refer to the act of waking up or becoming conscious after a period of sleep or unconsciousness.
