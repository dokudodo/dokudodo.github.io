Are you tired of being underweight and want to gain some healthy weight? Well, you've come to the right place! In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on a full day routine for weight gain. We will cover everything from the right foods to eat, the right exercises to do, and some tips and tricks to help you achieve your weight gain goals. So, let's get started!

Morning Routine

The first step to a successful full day routine for weight gain is to start your day off right. A good morning routine can set the tone for the rest of the day. Here are some things you can do:

  • Wake up at the same time every day
  • Drink a glass of water as soon as you wake up
  • Stretch or do some light yoga
  • Take a shower

Breakfast Ideas

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, especially for people who are trying to gain weight. Here are some breakfast ideas:

  • Eggs with whole wheat toast and avocado
  • Oatmeal with nuts and fruit
  • Smoothie with peanut butter, banana, and protein powder
  • Greek yogurt with granola and berries

Mid-Morning Snack

A mid-morning snack can help you maintain your energy levels until lunchtime. Here are some ideas:

  • Fruit with nut butter
  • Rice cakes with hummus
  • Cottage cheese with crackers
  • Protein bar or shake

Lunch Ideas

Lunch is another important meal, and it's essential to include a balance of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats. Here are some lunch ideas:

  • Grilled chicken with quinoa and roasted vegetables
  • Tuna salad with whole wheat crackers
  • Grilled cheese sandwich with tomato soup
  • Pasta with meat sauce and a side salad

Mid-Afternoon Snack

A mid-afternoon snack can help you maintain your energy levels until dinner time. Here are some ideas:

  • Trail mix with nuts and dried fruit
  • Celery with cream cheese or peanut butter
  • Apple slices with cheese
  • Protein shake or bar

Evening Routine

Having a good evening routine can help you wind down and prepare for a good night's sleep. Here are some things you can do:

  • Avoid electronics for at least 30 minutes before bed
  • Take a warm bath or shower
  • Read a book or listen to relaxing music

Dinner Ideas

Dinner is your last chance to fuel your body before bed, so it's essential to make it count. Here are some dinner ideas:

  • Baked salmon with sweet potato and green beans
  • Beef stir-fry with brown rice and vegetables
  • Lentil soup with a side salad
  • Grilled chicken with roasted vegetables and quinoa

Before Bed Snack

Having a small snack before bed can help you avoid waking up hungry in the middle of the night. Here are some ideas:

  • Greek yogurt with honey and nuts
  • Cottage cheese with sliced fruit
  • Protein shake or bar

Exercise Routine

Exercise is an essential part of any weight gain routine. Here are some exercises you can do:

  • Strength training with weights or resistance bands
  • Cardiovascular exercise like running or cycling
  • Yoga or Pilates

It's important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity of your workouts. Aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise per day.


Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you with your weight gain journey:

  • Eat frequent high-calorie meals and snacks throughout the day
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated
  • Avoid sugary and processed snacks
  • Get enough sleep every night
  • Track your progress and adjust your routine as needed

FAQs

1. How long does it take to gain weight?

Everyone's body is different, so it's hard to say exactly how long it will take to gain weight. However, with a consistent routine, you should start to see results within a few weeks to a few months.

2. Can I still eat junk food?

While it's important to focus on healthy, whole foods, you can still include some junk food in your diet in moderation. Just make sure you're not relying on it as your primary source of calories.

3. Will weight gain supplements help?

Weight gain supplements can be helpful, but they should not be relied on as your primary source of calories. It's important to focus on whole foods and a balanced diet first.

4. Can I gain weight without exercising?

While exercise is an essential part of a healthy weight gain routine, it's still possible to gain weight without it. However, exercise can help you build muscle and improve your overall health, so it's important to include it in your routine if possible.

In conclusion, a full day routine for weight gain involves eating frequent, high-calorie meals and snacks, exercising regularly, and getting enough sleep. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve your weight gain goals and improve your overall health.
