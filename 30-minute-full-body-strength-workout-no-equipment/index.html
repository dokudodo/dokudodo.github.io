How to:

This workout can be done anywhere, anytime, without any equipment needed. To get started, find a clear space that allows you to move freely. Wear comfortable clothing and shoes that provide support for your feet. It's also important to warm up before starting any exercise routine. Spend 5-10 minutes doing some light cardio, such as jogging in place, jumping jacks or high knees. This will increase your heart rate and warm up your muscles.

Step by Step:

Perform each exercise for 45 seconds, resting for 15 seconds in between each exercise. Complete 3 rounds of the following exercises:

1. Squat Jumps

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Lower down into a squat position, keeping your chest up and your weight in your heels. As you come up, jump explosively into the air, landing softly back into the squat position. Repeat for 45 seconds.
