If you are an early bird looking for things to do in London before the crowds arrive, you are in the right place! In this guide, we will explore some of the best activities to do in London in the early morning. From peaceful parks to historic sites, there are plenty of options to start your day right.

1. Explore Peaceful Parks

London has some of the most beautiful parks in the world, and visiting them in the early morning is a great way to start your day. Some of the most popular parks include Hyde Park, Regent's Park, and St. James's Park. You can take a stroll, do some yoga, or simply sit and enjoy the peaceful surroundings. The best part is that you will have the park almost entirely to yourself.

2. Stroll Through Markets

London is famous for its markets, and many of them open early in the morning. You can visit the Borough Market, which is known for its gourmet food, or the Columbia Road Flower Market, which is a feast for the senses. Walking through the markets in the early morning is a great way to experience the city's unique culture and get a head start on shopping.

3. Discover Historical Sites

London is steeped in history, and many of its most famous landmarks are located in the city center. You can visit the Tower of London, which was once a royal palace and is now a museum, or the Houses of Parliament, which are home to the iconic Big Ben clock tower. Visiting these sites in the early morning is a great way to avoid the crowds and take in the history of the city.

4. Enjoy a Delicious Breakfast

London has no shortage of great breakfast spots, and many of them open early in the morning. You can visit the Dishoom, which serves Indian-inspired breakfast dishes, or the Regency CafÃ©, which is a classic British diner. Eating breakfast in the early morning is a great way to fuel up for the day and get a taste of the local cuisine.

5. Get Some Exercise

If you are looking for a way to get some exercise in the early morning, London has plenty of options. You can take a jog through Hyde Park, go for a swim at the London Aquatics Centre, or try a yoga class at one of the many studios in the city. Getting some exercise in the early morning is a great way to start your day feeling energized and refreshed.

6. Take in the Views

London is known for its stunning views, and many of them are best seen in the early morning. You can visit the Sky Garden, which offers panoramic views of the city, or climb to the top of the Shard for an even more breathtaking view. Taking in the views in the early morning is a great way to appreciate the beauty of the city without the crowds.

7. Experience the Culture

London is home to some of the world's most famous museums and galleries, and many of them open early in the morning. You can visit the British Museum, which houses some of the most important historical artifacts in the world, or the Tate Modern, which showcases contemporary art. Visiting these cultural sites in the early morning is a great way to avoid the crowds and appreciate the art without distractions.

8. Shop Early

If you are a shopper, visiting the stores in the early morning is a great way to beat the crowds and get a head start on your shopping. You can visit Oxford Street, which is home to some of the most famous shops in the world, or Covent Garden, which is known for its unique boutiques. Shopping in the early morning is a great way to have the stores almost entirely to yourself.

9. Capture the City

If you are a photographer, visiting the city in the early morning is a great way to capture some of the most beautiful shots. You can visit the Tower Bridge, which is one of the most iconic landmarks in the city, or the Notting Hill neighborhood, which is famous for its colorful houses. Capturing the city in the early morning is a great way to appreciate its beauty without the crowds.

10. Find Some Relaxation

If you are looking for a way to relax in the early morning, London has plenty of options. You can visit a spa, such as the Agua Spa at the Mondrian London, or take a relaxing walk along the Thames. Finding some relaxation in the early morning is a great way to start your day feeling calm and centered.

Tips for Early Morning Activities in London

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your early morning activities in London:

  • Check the opening times before you go, as some attractions may not open until later in the morning.
  • Dress appropriately for the weather, as early mornings in London can be chilly.
  • Bring a camera to capture the beautiful sights.
  • Take advantage of the quiet streets and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Conclusion

London has plenty of options for early morning activities, from peaceful parks to historic sites to delicious breakfast spots. Whether you are a local or a visitor, exploring the city in the early morning is a great way to start your day feeling energized and refreshed. We hope this guide has inspired you to try some new activities and make the most of your time in London.
