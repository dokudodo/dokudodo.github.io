Things To Do In London Day Drinking A Guide

Written by Wednesday May 09, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re planning on spending a day in London and fancy a tipple or two, you’re in luck! London has some of the best bars and pubs in the world. In this guide, we’ll give you some tips on how to make the most of your day drinking experience in London. From rooftop bars to traditional pubs, we’ve got you covered. So grab a drink and let’s get started!

Rooftop Bars

Rooftop bars are a must-visit when it comes to day drinking in London. There’s nothing quite like sipping on a drink while taking in the stunning views of the city. Some of the best rooftop bars include Madison, which offers views of St. Paul’s Cathedral, and The Rooftop at The Trafalgar St. James, which offers views of Trafalgar Square. Other great options include the Sky Garden, which offers 360-degree views of the city, and the Boundary Rooftop, which offers views of the iconic Shard.

Traditional Pubs

If you’re looking for a more authentic British experience, traditional pubs are the way to go. These pubs often have a cozy atmosphere and serve up classic British dishes like fish and chips and bangers and mash. Some great traditional pubs to check out include The Churchill Arms, which is decorated with flowers and serves up Thai food alongside traditional pub fare, and The Princess Louise, which has a stunning Victorian interior.

Boat Bars

For a unique day drinking experience, why not try a boat bar? There are plenty of options to choose from, including the Tattershall Castle, which is a converted paddle steamer located on the Thames. Other options include the Bargehouse, which is located in Oxo Tower Wharf and offers stunning views of the city, and The Yacht London, which offers a more luxurious experience.

Brewery Tours

If you’re a beer lover, you won’t want to miss out on the many brewery tours available in London. Some of the best breweries to visit include the Camden Town Brewery, which offers tours and tastings, and the Fuller’s Brewery, which has been brewing beer for over 350 years. Other great options include the Beavertown Brewery, which offers tours and tastings in their taproom, and the Meantime Brewing Company, which offers tours and tastings in their Greenwich brewery.

Distillery Tours

If gin is more your thing, there are plenty of distillery tours to choose from in London. The Beefeater Gin Distillery offers tours and tastings, as well as the chance to create your own gin. The Sipsmith Distillery also offers tours and tastings, and the City of London Distillery offers gin-making classes. Other great options include the East London Liquor Company and the Jensen’s Gin Distillery.

Cocktail Bars

London is home to some of the best cocktail bars in the world. From speakeasies to swanky hotel bars, there’s something for everyone. Some great options include The Savoy’s American Bar, which has won numerous awards for its cocktails, and Nightjar, which is a speakeasy-style bar with live jazz music. Other great options include The Connaught Bar, which has a sophisticated Art Deco interior, and The Artesian, which is known for its innovative cocktail menu.

Food and Drink Pairings

If you’re looking to take your day drinking experience to the next level, why not try a food and drink pairing? Many bars and restaurants offer tasting menus that pair cocktails and wines with different dishes. Some great options include The Wine and Cheese Tasting at Gordon’s Wine Bar and The Cocktail and Dim Sum Pairing at Opium Cocktail and Dim Sum Parlour.

Events and Festivals

London is home to many events and festivals throughout the year that are perfect for day drinking. Some great options include the London Craft Beer Festival, which takes place in August, and the London Cocktail Week, which takes place in October. Other great options include the London Wine Week, the London Gin Festival, and the London Whisky Week.

Safety Tips

While day drinking can be a fun experience, it’s important to drink responsibly and stay safe. Make sure to pace yourself and drink plenty of water throughout the day. If you’re drinking outside, make sure to wear sunscreen and stay hydrated. It’s also important to keep an eye on your belongings and be aware of your surroundings.

Conclusion

London is a city that’s perfect for day drinking. With so many options to choose from, there’s something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for a rooftop bar with stunning views or a traditional pub with a cozy atmosphere, you won’t be disappointed. So grab a drink and enjoy all that London has to offer!
