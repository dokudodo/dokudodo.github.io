Description

When you're interviewing for a job, you may be asked the question, "What motivates you to get up in the morning?" This question is designed to assess your values, motivation, and your overall fit for the job.

Employers want to know what drives you, what inspires you, and what makes you tick. They're looking for people who are passionate about their work and who are excited to tackle new challenges. This question is an opportunity for you to show that you're that kind of person.

How to answer the question

The key to answering this question is to be honest and authentic. Don't try to guess what the employer wants to hear or give a generic answer. Think about what really motivates you and how it relates to the job you're applying for.

You want to show the employer that you're someone who is self-motivated and driven, but also someone who can align their personal goals with the company's goals.

Step-by-step guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to answer this question:

  1. Reflect on what motivates you. Think about your personal values, your interests, and your goals.
  2. Relate your motivations to the job you're applying for. How does this job align with your values and goals? What aspects of the job excite you?
  3. Be specific. Give concrete examples of what motivates you. For example, if you're motivated by helping others, talk about a time when you volunteered or helped someone in need.
  4. Show enthusiasm. Demonstrate that you're excited about the job and that you're motivated to do your best.

Tips to answer the question

Here are some tips to help you answer this question:

  • Be honest and authentic. Don't try to give a generic or fake answer.
  • Relate your answer to the job you're applying for.
  • Give concrete examples to support your answer.
  • Show enthusiasm and excitement.
  • Avoid negative or cynical answers.

Solution to the question

The solution to this question is to be true to yourself and to show the employer that you're someone who is self-motivated, driven, and passionate about your work. You want to demonstrate that you have a clear sense of purpose and that you're excited to tackle new challenges.

By being authentic and enthusiastic in your answer, you'll give the employer a glimpse into your personality and your work style. This can help them to determine if you're a good fit for the job and the company culture.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about this interview question:

Q: What should I avoid when answering this question?

A: You should avoid giving negative or cynical answers. Instead, focus on what motivates you and how it relates to the job you're applying for.

Q: Can I give a personal answer?

A: Yes, you can give a personal answer, but make sure to relate it to the job you're applying for.

Q: Should I research the company before answering this question?

A: Yes, it's a good idea to research the company and its values before answering this question. This can help you to align your motivations with the company's goals.

Pros and Cons of the question

Here are some pros and cons of this interview question:

Pros:

  • Allows the employer to assess your values and motivation.
  • Gives you an opportunity to showcase your personality and work style.
  • Can help to determine if you're a good fit for the job and company culture.

Cons:

  • Can be a difficult question to answer if you're not prepared.
  • May require some self-reflection and introspection.
  • Can reveal personal information that you may not want to share.

Overall, the "What motivates you to get up in the morning?" interview question is an opportunity for you to show your passion, drive, and alignment with the company's goals. By being honest, authentic, and enthusiastic in your answer, you can set yourself apart from other candidates and demonstrate that you're the right fit for the job.
