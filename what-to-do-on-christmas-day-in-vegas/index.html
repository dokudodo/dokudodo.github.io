What To Do On Christmas Day In Vegas A Guide

Are you spending Christmas Day in Las Vegas this year? Don’t worry, there are still plenty of things to do even if you’re away from home. This guide will give you some ideas on how to make the most of your Christmas Day in Sin City.

1. Visit the Christmas Decorations at the Bellagio

The Bellagio is known for its stunning Christmas decorations, including a 42-foot-tall Christmas tree made entirely of glass. Take a stroll through the lobby and conservatory to see the elaborate holiday displays. Don't forget to take a photo in front of the tree!

2. Go Ice Skating at the Cosmopolitan

The Cosmopolitan offers an ice skating rink on its rooftop. It’s a great way to get into the holiday spirit while enjoying the beautiful view of the Las Vegas skyline. Skating sessions start at $20 per person, and it’s open from noon to midnight on Christmas Day.

3. Enjoy a Christmas Buffet

Vegas is known for its amazing buffets, and Christmas Day is no exception. Many of the hotels and casinos offer special holiday buffets with traditional Christmas dishes like turkey and ham. Be sure to make a reservation ahead of time to avoid long lines.

4. Take a Drive to the Red Rock Canyon

If you’re looking for a break from the hustle and bustle of the city, take a drive to the Red Rock Canyon. The scenic drive offers stunning views of the red rock formations, and you can even go for a hike if you’re feeling adventurous.

5. Watch a Holiday Show

Many of the casinos and theaters offer special holiday shows on Christmas Day. From classic Christmas carols to modern dance performances, there’s something for everyone. Check the schedule ahead of time and book your tickets in advance.

6. Play Games at an Arcade

There are plenty of arcades in Vegas that offer fun and games for all ages. From classic arcade games to virtual reality experiences, you can spend hours playing and having fun.

7. Take a Stroll on the Strip

The Las Vegas Strip is always buzzing with activity, and Christmas Day is no exception. Take a stroll down the Strip and check out the holiday lights and decorations. You never know what kind of street performers or entertainers you’ll come across.

8. Visit a Museum

Vegas has a variety of museums that are open on Christmas Day. From the Mob Museum to the Neon Museum, there’s something for everyone. It’s a great way to spend a few hours learning something new and interesting.

9. Take a Helicopter Tour of the City

For a unique and unforgettable experience, take a helicopter tour of the city. You’ll get a bird’s eye view of the Strip and the surrounding area. It’s a great way to see the city from a different perspective.

10. Relax at the Spa

After a busy holiday season, why not treat yourself to a relaxing day at the spa? Many of the hotels and casinos offer spa packages that include massages, facials, and other treatments. It’s the perfect way to unwind and recharge.

Conclusion

With so many options, there’s no reason to feel bored on Christmas Day in Vegas. Whether you want to go ice skating or visit a museum, there’s something for everyone. Just remember to plan ahead and make reservations when necessary. Have a happy and memorable holiday!
