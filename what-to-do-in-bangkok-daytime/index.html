What To Do In Bangkok Daytime A Complete Guide

Are you planning a trip to Bangkok and wondering what to do during the daytime? We’ve got you covered! In this guide, we will provide you with a list of activities and places to visit in Bangkok during the day. From temples to markets, there's something for everyone. Let's get started!

Visit Temples

Bangkok is known for its beautiful temples, and visiting them during the day is a must-do activity. One of the most famous temples is the Grand Palace, which is home to the Temple of the Emerald Buddha. Other popular temples include Wat Phra Kaew, Wat Pho, and Wat Arun. Make sure to dress modestly and respectfully when visiting temples.

Explore Markets

Bangkok is also known for its vibrant markets. From the famous Chatuchak Weekend Market to the floating markets, there's something for everyone. You can find everything from traditional Thai handicrafts to exotic fruits and street food. Make sure to bring cash and haggle for the best prices.

Relax in Parks

If you're looking for a break from the hustle and bustle of the city, Bangkok has plenty of parks where you can relax and enjoy nature. One of the most popular parks is Lumpini Park, which is home to a lake, jogging paths, and outdoor exercise equipment. Other parks include Benjasiri Park, King Rama IX Park, and Chatuchak Park.

Discover Museums

Bangkok has a rich history and culture, and there are plenty of museums where you can learn about it. The Bangkok National Museum is the largest museum in Southeast Asia and showcases Thai art, history, and culture. Other museums include the Jim Thompson House, the Museum of Siam, and the Erawan Museum.

Indulge in Food

Bangkok is known for its delicious street food, and trying it during the day is a must-do activity. From Pad Thai to Mango Sticky Rice, there's something for everyone. You can also visit restaurants and cafes that serve traditional Thai dishes like Tom Yum and Green Curry. Make sure to try a Thai Iced Tea or Coffee as well.

Go Shopping

Shopping in Bangkok is a popular activity, and there are plenty of malls and shopping centers to visit during the day. Siam Paragon, CentralWorld, and MBK Center are some of the most popular malls. You can also visit luxury shopping centers like Gaysorn Village and EmQuartier. Make sure to bring your shopping bags!

Take a Boat Tour

Bangkok is known as the “Venice of the East” because of its extensive network of canals or “khlongs”. Taking a boat tour during the day is a unique way to explore the city. You can take a long-tail boat or a river cruise and see famous landmarks like Wat Arun and the Grand Palace from the water.

Treat Yourself to a Spa Day

If you're looking for some relaxation and pampering, Bangkok has plenty of spas to choose from. You can indulge in a traditional Thai massage or try other treatments like herbal compress, hot stone massage, and aromatherapy. Some of the most popular spas include Health Land Spa, Let's Relax Spa, and Oasis Spa.

Visit Art Galleries

Bangkok has a thriving art scene, and there are plenty of galleries to visit during the day. The Bangkok Art and Culture Centre is a contemporary art museum that showcases Thai and international artists. Other galleries include the National Gallery, the River City Bangkok, and the H Gallery Bangkok.

Prepare for Nightlife

While this guide is focused on daytime activities, it's important to mention Bangkok's famous nightlife. If you're planning to go out at night, make sure to prepare beforehand. Research the best clubs, bars, and rooftop bars, and make sure to dress appropriately. Some of the most popular nightlife areas include Khao San Road, Soi Cowboy, and Thonglor.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of things to do in Bangkok during the day. From temples to markets, parks to museums, there's something for everyone. Make sure to plan your itinerary beforehand, and don't forget to bring a camera to capture all the amazing sights. Happy traveling!
