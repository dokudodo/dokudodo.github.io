Description

Many people are curious about how many calories they burn without exercise per day. This number is important to know because it can help you maintain a healthy weight or lose weight by making small changes to your diet or lifestyle. Your basal metabolic rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body burns at rest to maintain basic functions like breathing, circulation, and digestion. This rate varies based on factors like age, gender, weight, and height.

How To

To determine your BMR, you can use an online calculator that takes into account your age, gender, weight, and height. This will give you an estimate of how many calories you burn without exercise per day. Keep in mind that this is just an estimate and your actual BMR may be slightly different. It's also important to note that your BMR decreases as you age, so you may need to adjust your calorie intake over time.

Step By Step

  1. Find an online BMR calculator that takes into account your age, gender, weight, and height.
  2. Enter your information into the calculator.
  3. The calculator will give you an estimate of how many calories you burn without exercise per day.

Tips

If you're trying to lose weight, you can create a calorie deficit by consuming fewer calories than your BMR. However, it's important to make sure you're still consuming enough calories to meet your body's nutritional needs. Eating too few calories can be harmful to your health.

You can also increase your BMR by building muscle through strength training exercises. Muscle burns more calories than fat, so the more muscle you have, the more calories you'll burn at rest.

Solution

If you're looking to maintain a healthy weight or lose weight, knowing your BMR can be a helpful tool. By making small changes to your diet or lifestyle, you can create a calorie deficit and achieve your weight loss goals. However, it's important to make sure you're still consuming enough calories to meet your body's nutritional needs.
