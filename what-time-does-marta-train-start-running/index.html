What time does Marta train start running?

Marta's trains start running at 5:00 am on weekdays and Saturdays, and at 6:00 am on Sundays and holidays. The last train departs at 1:00 am on weekdays and Saturdays, and at 12:00 am on Sundays and holidays.

It's important to note that these times are subject to change, so it's always a good idea to check Marta's website or app for the most up-to-date information.

How to use Marta

Marta is a convenient and affordable way to get around Atlanta. To use Marta, you'll need to purchase a Breeze Card, which is a reusable card that you can load with money or passes. Breeze Cards can be purchased at Marta stations or online.

Once you have a Breeze Card, you can use it to ride Marta's trains and buses. Simply tap your card on the card reader when you enter and exit the train or bus. The fare varies depending on how far you're traveling and what time of day it is.

Step by step guide to using Marta

Here's a step-by-step guide to using Marta:

  1. Purchase a Breeze Card at a Marta station or online.
  2. Load money or passes onto your Breeze Card.
  3. Tap your Breeze Card on the card reader when you enter and exit the train or bus.
  4. Follow the signs to your train or bus.
  5. Find a seat or hold onto a handrail or strap.
  6. Get off at your stop.
  7. Tap your Breeze Card on the card reader when you exit the train or bus.

Tips for using Marta

Here are some tips for using Marta:

  • Check the schedule and plan your route ahead of time.
  • Buy a weekly or monthly pass if you're using Marta frequently.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and keep your belongings close to you.
  • Stand to the right on escalators and stairs to allow others to pass on the left.
  • Be patient and courteous to other riders.

Frequently asked questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about Marta:

  • Is Marta safe?
  • Yes, Marta is safe. However, like any public transportation system, it's important to be aware of your surroundings and keep your belongings close to you.
  • Can I bring a bike on Marta?
  • Yes, you can bring a bike on Marta. However, bikes are only allowed on certain trains and buses during certain times of day. Check Marta's website for more information.
  • Can I bring food or drink on Marta?
  • Yes, you can bring food and drink on Marta. However, be considerate of other riders and clean up after yourself.

Pros and cons of using Marta

Here are some pros and cons of using Marta:

Pros:

  • Convenient and affordable way to get around Atlanta
  • Accessible to many areas of the city
  • Reliable and safe

Cons:

  • Some areas may not be easily accessible by Marta
