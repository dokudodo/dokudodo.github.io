Introduction

Life can be tough, and sometimes we need a little motivation to keep us going. Whether you're trying to achieve a goal, overcome a challenge, or just get through the day, these best motivational quotes in English with Hindi meaning can provide the perfect inspiration.

Each of these quotes is powerful on its own, but when paired with its Hindi translation, the message becomes even more impactful. So, whether you speak English, Hindi, or both, these quotes are sure to inspire and motivate you.

Quote 1

"Believe you can and you're halfway there." - Theodore Roosevelt

"विश्वास करो कि तुम कर सकते हो, और आधा काम तो कर लिया।" - थियोडोर रूज़वेल्ट

This quote reminds us that belief in ourselves is key to achieving our goals. When we have confidence in our abilities, we are more likely to take action and make progress.

Quote 2

"You miss 100% of the shots you don't take." - Wayne Gretzky

"वह क्षण वापस नहीं आता जो आपने मौका नहीं दिया।" - वेन ग्रेट्ज्की

This quote is a reminder that we need to take risks in order to achieve our goals. If we never try, we'll never know what we're capable of.

Quote 3

"Success is not final, failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts." - Winston Churchill

"सफलता अंतिम नहीं है, असफलता जानलेवा नहीं है: जारी रखने की हिम्मत ही गिनती करती है।" - विंस्टन चर्चिल

This quote reminds us that success and failure are both temporary. What matters most is our ability to keep going, even when things get tough.

Quote 4

"Don't watch the clock; do what it does. Keep going." - Sam Levenson

"घड़ी को देखने की बजाय वह काम करो जो घड़ी करती है। जारी रखो।" - सैम लेवेंसन

This quote reminds us to focus on our work and keep going, rather than getting bogged down by time constraints or distractions.

Quote 5

"I have not failed. I've just found 10,000 ways that won't work." - Thomas Edison

"मैं असफल नहीं हुआ। मैंने बस 10,000 तरीकों को खोज लिया है जो काम नहीं करते।" - थॉमस एडिसन

This quote reminds us that failure is not the end, but rather a stepping stone to success. Every failure is an opportunity to learn and grow.

Quote 6

"You are never too old to set another goal or to dream a new dream." - C.S. Lewis

"एक और लक्ष्य निर्धारित करने के लिए या एक नया सपना देखने के लिए कभी भी ज्यादा बड़ा नहीं हो जाते।" - सी.एस. लूइस

This quote reminds us that it's never too late to pursue our dreams and set new goals. Age should never be a barrier to achieving what we want in life.

Quote 7

"The only way to do great work is to love what you do." - Steve Jobs

"बड़े काम करने का एकमात्र तरीका वह काम करना है जो तुम पूरे दिल से प्यार करते हो।" - स्टीव जॉब्स

This quote reminds us that passion for our work is essential for achieving greatness. When we love what we do, we are more likely to put in the effort and excel.

Quote 8

"If you want to achieve greatness, stop asking for permission." - Unknown

"अगर तुम महानता हासिल करना चाहते हो, तो इजाजत माँगना बंद करो।" - अज्ञात

This quote reminds us that we don't need permission from others to achieve our goals. We have the power to make our own decisions and take action towards success.

Quote 9

"Believe in yourself and all that you are. Know that there is something inside you that is greater than any obstacle." - Christian D. Larson

"खुद पर विश्वास करो और उस सब कुछ पर जो तुम हो। जानो कि तुम्हारे अंदर कुछ ऐसा है जो किसी भी बाधा से बड़ा है।" - क्रिस्टियान डी लार्सन

This quote reminds us that we have the power within ourselves to overcome any obstacle. Believing in ourselves is the first step towards achieving our goals.

Quote 10

"The best way to predict the future is to create it." - Abraham Lincoln

"भविष्य का सबसे अच्छा अनुमान उसे बनाना है।" - एब्राहम लिंकन

This quote reminds us that we have the power to shape our own future. By taking action and working towards our goals, we can create the future we want for ourselves.

Conclusion

These best motivational quotes in English with Hindi meaning are just a few examples of the powerful words that can inspire and motivate us
