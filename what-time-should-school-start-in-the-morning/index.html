What Time Should School Start In The Morning

Are you a student, parent, or educator wondering what time school should start in the morning? The debate over school start times has been ongoing for years, with studies showing the benefits of later start times for students. In this article, we will explore the best time for school to start and the reasons behind it.

Why is the Start Time of School Important?

The start time of school affects many aspects of a child's life, including their academic performance, physical health, and mental wellbeing. Research shows that children who start school later in the morning have better academic performance, improved attendance, and are more likely to graduate high school.

Furthermore, starting school later can improve a child's physical health by allowing them to get enough sleep. Lack of sleep has been linked to obesity, diabetes, and other health problems in children.

How to Determine the Best Start Time for School?

The ideal start time for school varies depending on the student's age and individual needs. Younger children may benefit from starting school earlier in the morning, while teenagers may benefit from starting later.

Experts suggest that elementary school should start between 7:30 am and 8:30 am, while middle and high school should start between 8:30 am and 9:30 am. This allows younger children to get home earlier in the afternoon and gives teenagers enough time to get the sleep they need.

Step-by-Step Guide to Determining the Best Start Time for Your School

1. Assess your student population: Take into account the age range and individual needs of your students.

2. Review research on ideal start times: Look for studies that have been conducted on school start times and their impact on student performance and health.

3. Survey parents and students: Gather feedback from parents and students on what start time would work best for them.

4. Consult with educators and healthcare professionals: Get input from educators and healthcare professionals on what start time would be best for students' academic and physical needs.

Tips for Implementing a New Start Time

Changing the start time of school can be a challenging process. Here are some tips to help make the transition smoother:

- Communicate the change clearly and frequently to parents, students, and staff members.

- Provide resources and support to help students adjust to the new schedule.

- Gradually shift the start time over a period of weeks to help students and staff adjust to the new schedule.

Pros and Cons of Starting School Later

Pros

- Improved academic performance

- Better attendance

- Improved physical health

- Reduced tardiness

Cons

- Increased transportation costs

- Conflicts with after-school activities and jobs

- Difficulty in accommodating different family schedules

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Elementary school should start between 7:30 am and 8:30 am.

What time should middle and high school start?

Middle and high school should start between 8:30 am and 9:30 am.

What are the benefits of starting school later?

Starting school later can improve academic performance, attendance, physical health, and mental wellbeing.

What are the challenges of changing the start time of school?

Challenges include increased transportation costs, conflicts with after-school activities and jobs, and difficulty in accommodating different family schedules.

In conclusion, determining the best start time for school requires careful consideration of the individual needs of students. Starting school later can have many benefits, but it also presents challenges. By following the steps outlined in this article and considering the pros and cons, schools can make informed decisions about the start time that will benefit their students the most.
