Quotes for Teacher in English

Welcome to our blog post about Quotes for Teacher in English. Teachers are an integral part of our lives and they deserve appreciation for their hard work and dedication. In this post, we will be sharing some of the best quotes for teachers in English that you can use to express your gratitude towards them. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Quote 1
  • Quote 2
  • Quote 3
  • Quote 4

Quote 1

"A teacher affects eternity; he can never tell where his influence stops." - Henry Adams

This quote emphasizes the impact that a teacher has on their students. The knowledge and values that a teacher imparts to their students can last a lifetime.


Quote 2

"The mediocre teacher tells. The good teacher explains. The superior teacher demonstrates. The great teacher inspires." - William Arthur Ward

This quote highlights the qualities that make a great teacher. A great teacher not only imparts knowledge but also inspires their students to be better.


Quote 3

"The art of teaching is the art of assisting discovery." - Mark Van Doren

This quote emphasizes the role of a teacher in helping their students discover their own potential. A good teacher guides their students towards their own discoveries and helps them realize their own abilities.


Quote 4

"The best teachers are those who show you where to look but don't tell you what to see." - Alexandra K. Trenfor

This quote emphasizes the importance of allowing students to discover things for themselves. A great teacher doesn't dictate what their students should know but instead guides them towards finding the answers themselves.


Quote 5

"Teaching is a very noble profession that shapes the character, caliber, and future of an individual. If the people remember me as a good teacher, that will be the biggest honour for me." - A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

This quote highlights the significance of the teaching profession in shaping the future of individuals. A good teacher has the power to transform the lives of their students and leave a lasting impact on them.


Conclusion

We hope that these quotes for teacher in English have inspired you to appreciate the hard work and dedication of your teachers. Remember to express your gratitude towards them and let them know how much they mean to you.
