Indoor Activities For 12 Year Olds Keep Your Kids Entertained And Engaged

At the age of 12, kids are in their pre-teen years and are trying to explore different things. It’s hard to keep them entertained and engaged, especially when they are stuck indoors. But don’t worry, we have got you covered! In this blog post, we will share some fun and exciting indoor activities for 12 year olds that will keep them busy and happy.

Game Night

Game night is a great way to bond with your kids and have fun. You can play board games, card games, or even video games. There are many games that are suitable for 12 year olds such as Monopoly, Clue, and Uno. You can also try some new games like Settlers of Catan or Pandemic. Make sure to have some snacks and drinks ready for a complete experience.

Art and Crafts

Art and crafts are a great way to unleash your kids’ creativity. You can try painting, drawing, or even making DIY projects. You can also try some fun crafts like making friendship bracelets, paper mache, or origami. There are plenty of online resources available to get inspiration and ideas for art and crafts.

Cooking Challenge

Cooking is a life skill that every kid should learn. You can challenge your kids to cook a meal or a dessert. You can also have a cooking competition where they have to make a dish using a specific ingredient or theme. This is a great way to teach them about nutrition, measurement, and planning.

Movie Marathon

A movie marathon is a great way to spend a lazy day indoors. You can choose a theme or a genre and watch movies back to back. You can also make some popcorn, have some snacks, and make it a cozy experience. Some great movie ideas for 12 year olds are Harry Potter, The Hunger Games, and Spiderman.

Virtual Tours

Virtual tours are a great way to explore different places without leaving your home. You can visit museums, national parks, or even go on a safari. Many museums and places offer virtual tours for free, and you can learn a lot about history, science, and culture.

Karaoke Night

Karaoke is a fun activity that can bring out your kids’ confidence and creativity. You can buy a karaoke machine or use an app on your phone. You can also make it a competition and have everyone vote for the best performance. This is a great way to have fun and make memories.

Board Games

Board games are a classic indoor activity that never gets old. You can choose from a variety of board games such as Chess, Checkers, Scrabble, or Risk. You can also try some new games like Ticket to Ride or Carcassonne. Board games are a great way to improve critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Science Experiments

Science experiments are a fun way to learn about science and explore different concepts. You can try some simple experiments like making a volcano or growing crystals. You can also try some more advanced experiments like building a robot or making a solar oven. There are plenty of online resources available to get ideas and instructions for science experiments.

Exercise

Exercise is important for kids’ physical and mental health. You can try some fun indoor exercises like yoga, dance, or aerobics. You can also make it a family activity and have everyone participate. Exercise is a great way to release energy and reduce stress.

Conclusion

Indoor activities for 12 year olds can be fun and engaging. You can try some of these activities to keep your kids entertained and happy. Game night, art and crafts, cooking challenge, movie marathon, virtual tours, karaoke night, board games, science experiments, and exercise are some great ideas to try. Remember to have fun and bond with your kids while doing these activities.
