If you're looking for a quick and effective way to tone your core, then you may have heard of Lilly Sabri's 5 min abs workout. This workout has become increasingly popular over the years, and for good reason. With just 5 minutes a day, you can achieve a toned and strong core. In this article, we'll take a closer look at Lilly Sabri's 5 min abs workout and the results you can expect.

Description

Lilly Sabri is a certified personal trainer and fitness influencer who has gained a massive following on social media. She created the 5 min abs workout to help people get a toned core without spending hours at the gym. The workout consists of five exercises, each done for 45 seconds with a 15-second rest in between. The exercises target all areas of the core, including the upper and lower abs, obliques, and lower back.

How to

The 5 min abs workout is easy to follow and can be done anywhere, anytime. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Warm-up

Before you start the workout, it's important to warm up your body. You can do a few minutes of light cardio, such as jogging in place or jumping jacks, to get your blood flowing.

Step 2: Set a timer

Set a timer for 5 minutes and get ready to start the workout.

Step 3: Do the exercises

Perform each exercise for 45 seconds, with a 15-second rest in between. Here are the five exercises:

  1. Crunches
  2. Bicycle crunches
  3. Mountain climbers
  4. Plank twists
  5. Reverse crunches

Step 4: Cool-down

After you finish the workout, take a few minutes to cool down. You can stretch your core muscles or do some light cardio to bring your heart rate down.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of Lilly Sabri's 5 min abs workout:

  • Do the workout every day for best results
  • Focus on proper form to avoid injury
  • Increase the intensity by adding weights or doing more repetitions
  • Combine the workout with a healthy diet for even better results

Solution

The 5 min abs workout is a great solution for people who want to get a toned core but don't have a lot of time to spare. With just 5 minutes a day, you can achieve a stronger and more defined core. The workout is also easy to follow and requires no equipment, making it accessible to everyone.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about Lilly Sabri's 5 min abs workout:

Q: How many calories can you burn with the 5 min abs workout?

A: The number of calories you burn depends on your weight, age, and fitness level. However, you can expect to burn around 50-100 calories in 5 minutes.

Q: Can you do the workout at any time of the day?

A: Yes, you can do the workout at any time of the day that suits you best. However, it's best to do it when you have the most energy, such as in the morning or early afternoon.

Q: Is the 5 min abs workout suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, the workout is suitable for beginners. However, it's important to start with proper form and gradually increase the intensity as you get stronger.

Pros and Cons

Here are some pros and cons of Lilly Sabri's 5 min abs workout:

Pros:

  • Quick and effective
  • No equipment needed
  • Targets all areas of the core
  • Easy to follow

Cons:

  • May not be enough for people who want more challenging workouts
  • Requires consistency for best results
  • May not be suitable for people with certain medical conditions

In conclusion, Lilly Sabri's 5 min abs workout is a great way to get a toned core in just 5 minutes a day. The workout is easy to follow, requires no equipment, and targets all areas of the core. By combining the workout with a healthy diet and consistent exercise, you can achieve even better results.
