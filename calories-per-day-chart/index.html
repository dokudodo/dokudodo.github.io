Calories Per Day Chart How To Use It In Your Diet Plan

Are you trying to lose weight or maintain your current weight? One of the most important things you can do is track your daily calorie intake. But how many calories should you be consuming each day? And how can you keep track of them? This is where a calories per day chart can be helpful. In this article, we’ll explain what a calories per day chart is, how to use it, and some tips for success.

What is a Calories Per Day Chart?

A calories per day chart is a tool that helps you track your daily calorie intake. It provides a recommended range of calories based on your age, gender, weight, height, and activity level. This information can be used to plan meals and snacks throughout the day, ensuring that you stay within your recommended calorie range.

How is the Recommended Calorie Range Determined?

The recommended calorie range is determined by taking into account several factors, including:

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Weight
  • Height
  • Activity level

For example, a sedentary 30-year-old female who weighs 150 pounds and is 5’4” tall would have a recommended calorie range of 1,600-1,800 calories per day. This range would increase if she were more active or decrease if she were less active.

How to Use a Calories Per Day Chart

Using a calories per day chart is simple. First, determine your recommended calorie range based on your age, gender, weight, height, and activity level. Then, use a food journal or app to track your daily calorie intake.

When tracking your calories, it’s important to be as accurate as possible. Use measuring cups and a food scale to measure your portions, and check the nutrition labels on packaged foods.

At the end of each day, compare your calorie intake to your recommended calorie range. If you’re within your range, great! If not, consider making adjustments to your diet or activity level.

Example:

Let’s say you’re a 40-year-old male who is 6’0” tall and weighs 200 pounds. You’re moderately active, so your recommended calorie range is 2,200-2,400 calories per day.

For breakfast, you have a bowl of oatmeal with almond milk, a banana, and a cup of coffee with creamer. This adds up to about 400 calories.

For lunch, you have a turkey and cheese sandwich on whole wheat bread with a side salad and a glass of water. This adds up to about 500 calories.

For dinner, you have grilled chicken with roasted vegetables and a baked sweet potato. This adds up to about 600 calories.

Throughout the day, you snack on a handful of almonds, an apple, and a protein bar. This adds up to about 400 calories.

At the end of the day, your total calorie intake is 1,900 calories, which is within your recommended range.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips for using a calories per day chart:

  • Be honest with yourself about what you’re eating. Don’t underestimate portion sizes or forget to track snacks.
  • Use a food journal or app to make tracking easier.
  • Plan your meals and snacks ahead of time to ensure that you stay within your recommended calorie range.
  • Don’t skip meals in an attempt to “save” calories for later. This can lead to overeating later on.
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day with water or other low-calorie beverages.
  • Find a form of exercise that you enjoy and make it a regular part of your routine.

FAQ

What if I’m not sure about my recommended calorie range?

You can use an online calculator or consult with a registered dietitian to determine your recommended calorie range.

Can I still eat my favorite foods while using a calories per day chart?

Yes! The key is to practice moderation and balance. You can still enjoy your favorite foods, but be mindful of portion sizes and frequency of consumption.

How long will it take to see results?

This varies from person to person, but if you stick to your recommended calorie range and incorporate regular exercise, you should see results within a few weeks to a few months.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Helps you stay within your recommended calorie range.
  • Provides a clear picture of your daily calorie intake.
  • Can be used to plan meals and snacks ahead of time.
  • Encourages mindful eating and portion control.

Cons:

  • Can be time-consuming to track calories.
  • May feel restrictive or tedious to some people.
  • Doesn’t take into account individual dietary needs or preferences.

In conclusion, a calories per day chart can be a helpful tool for tracking your daily calorie intake and staying within your recommended calorie range. By using the chart in combination with a balanced diet and regular exercise, you can achieve your weight loss or maintenance goals.
