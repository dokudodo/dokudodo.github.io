Push Day Workout For Muscle Gain A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Jordan Apr 16, 2023 · 4 min read

.

If you are looking to build muscle and get stronger, then incorporating a push day workout into your routine is a must. This type of workout focuses on working the muscles in the upper body, specifically the chest, shoulders, and triceps. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on push day workout for muscle gain, including how to do it, step by step guide, tips, solution, FAQ, pros, and cons.

Table of Contents

  • What is Push Day Workout?
  • How to Do Push Day Workout?
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Success
  • Solution to Common Problems
  • FAQ

What is Push Day Workout?

Push day workout is a type of resistance training that focuses on working the muscles in the upper body. This workout usually includes exercises such as bench press, shoulder press, and tricep dips. The primary goal of this workout is to build strength and muscle mass in the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

How to Do Push Day Workout?

To do a push day workout, you will need to focus on exercises that target the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Here are some of the best exercises to include in your push day workout:
  • Bench press
  • Shoulder press
  • Tricep dips
  • Incline bench press
  • Push-ups

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to perform a push day workout:
  1. Warm up for 10-15 minutes with light cardio and stretching exercises
  2. Start with the bench press and do 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  3. Move on to the shoulder press and do 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  4. Do 3 sets of tricep dips with 10-12 reps
  5. Finish with incline bench press and push-ups with 3 sets of 10-12 reps each
  6. Cool down with some light stretching exercises

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your push day workout:
  • Start with lighter weights and gradually increase the weight as you get stronger
  • Focus on proper form and technique to avoid injuries
  • Rest for 60-90 seconds between sets to allow your muscles to recover
  • Incorporate other upper body exercises into your workout routine to add variety and prevent boredom
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in protein to support muscle growth and recovery

Solution to Common Problems

Here are some solutions to common problems people face when doing push day workout:
  • If you experience pain in your shoulders during the shoulder press, try using lighter weights or switching to a different exercise that targets the same muscles
  • If you are struggling to complete the recommended number of reps, try decreasing the weight or taking a longer rest period between sets
  • If you are not seeing results, try increasing the weight or adding more sets and reps to your workout

FAQ

Q. How often should I do push day workout?

A. You can do push day workout 1-2 times per week, depending on your fitness level and goals.

Q. Can push day workout help me lose weight?

A. While push day workout can help you build muscle and increase strength, it is not specifically geared towards weight loss. To lose weight, you need to incorporate a combination of cardio and strength training exercises into your routine.

Pros and Cons

Here are some pros and cons of doing push day workout:

Pros

  • Builds strength and muscle mass in the upper body
  • Increases overall fitness and endurance
  • Can be done with minimal equipment

Cons

  • May cause muscle soreness and fatigue
  • Requires proper form and technique to avoid injuries
  • Can be challenging for beginners
In conclusion, push day workout is an effective way to build strength and muscle mass in the upper body. By following the tips and guidelines provided in this article, you can create a workout routine that will help you achieve your fitness goals. Remember to start slow, focus on proper technique, and gradually increase the weight and intensity of your workouts over time.
