Things To Do On Christmas Day Melbourne

Christmas Day in Melbourne is a wonderful time to get together with family and friends and celebrate the festive season. If you're looking for some ideas on how to spend your day, we've got you covered. Here are some fun things to do on Christmas Day in Melbourne:

1. Enjoy a Christmas Day lunch

One of the most popular things to do on Christmas Day in Melbourne is to enjoy a Christmas Day lunch. Many restaurants and hotels around the city offer special festive menus and events on this day. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disappointment.

2. Take a stroll through the city

Another great way to spend Christmas Day in Melbourne is to take a leisurely stroll through the city. Enjoy the festive decorations and lights, and take in the sights and sounds of the city. It's a great way to burn off some of those Christmas calories!

3. Visit the Queen Victoria Market

The Queen Victoria Market is a popular destination for locals and tourists alike, and it's open on Christmas Day! You can browse the stalls and pick up some last-minute Christmas gifts, or simply enjoy the festive atmosphere.

4. Watch a movie

Christmas Day is a great day to catch up on some movies. Many cinemas around Melbourne are open on Christmas Day, so why not head to the movies with family and friends?

5. Take a dip at the beach

Christmas Day in Melbourne is known for being hot and sunny, so why not take advantage of the weather and head to the beach? St Kilda beach is a popular spot for locals and tourists alike, and it's a great place to cool off.

6. Have a picnic in the park

If you're looking for a more relaxed way to spend Christmas Day in Melbourne, why not pack a picnic and head to one of the city's many parks? The Botanic Gardens and Fitzroy Gardens are both popular spots for picnics.

7. Go on a Christmas lights tour

Many neighbourhoods in Melbourne go all out with their Christmas lights displays, and going on a Christmas lights tour is a fun way to spend the evening. There are many companies that offer guided tours, or you can create your own route.

8. Play some board games

If you're spending Christmas Day with family and friends, why not break out some board games? It's a fun way to spend time together and enjoy some friendly competition.

9. Volunteer

Christmas is a time for giving, and volunteering is a great way to give back to the community. There are many organisations in Melbourne that need volunteers on Christmas Day, so why not lend a hand?

10. Relax at home

Finally, if you're looking for a more low-key way to spend Christmas Day in Melbourne, why not just relax at home? Put on some Christmas movies, cook a festive feast, and spend time with loved ones.

In Conclusion

Christmas Day in Melbourne is a time to celebrate with family and friends, and there are plenty of fun things to do around the city. Whether you're looking for a festive lunch or a relaxed day at home, there's something for everyone. We hope these ideas have inspired you to make the most of your Christmas Day in Melbourne!
